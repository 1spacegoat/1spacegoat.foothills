Safety Advisors

Foothills Safety Solutions is in place to work within your operation. Our Corporate HSE advisers are experts in Regulatory Compliance, Safety Management Program development and auditing. As well as Certificate of Recognition (COR) auditors. Our site Safety Supervisors are skilled, trained professionals with decades of industry experience that will blend in with the onsite team. We are not "Safety Cops" looking for a reason to justify our jobs with a checklist. Our Safety Supervisors will assist crews in identifying hazards, reviewing procedures, leading safety meetings and ensuring that documented procedures are followed, with a focus to ensure unsafe behaviors are recognized and addressed before an incident could occur
