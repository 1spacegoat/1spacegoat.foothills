About US

Foothills Safety Solutions founded in 2011 is an innovative and diverse group of professionals with years of field experience in the Canadian industry and abroad. Our goal is to help our clients develop their safety culture and ensure that everyone who works at their sites understands and utilizes the highest standards of safety possible. Collectively, we offer a variety of services from Site Safety Supervisors to Work Site inspections / audits for every size of business.

Innovation

Foothills Safety Solutions developed it's own software and web application to bring a more efficient and dependable use of technology to an increasingly demanding environment. We have the people to carry out the work and the ability to develop the technology that will make our client's workload easier to manage.

Corporate Values

  • Leadership- Technological innovation and the use of highly skilled professionals
  • Mentoring-Communication and evaluation
  • Quality-Continually striving to improve our client and employee satisfaction

Safety Culture

Foothills Safety Solutions goal is to work with our clients to not only develop a Safety Culture at their job sites but also promote a safety conscious environment for all workers on or off the site.

Accountability

Foothills Safety Solutions was developed to serve your business with a superior level of service, accountability and uncompromising integrity through trained field hands, use of new technology and years of service in the Canadian oilfield.
